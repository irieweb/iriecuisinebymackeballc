Our Story

Irie Cuisine By Mackeba was born from a deep love of Jamaican cooking and a dream to share those bold, soulful flavors with the community. What started as a family passion has grown into a rolling kitchen bringing real-deal Caribbean food to the streets of Bunnell, Florida.

Every dish we serve carries the spirit of the islands — from our signature jerk chicken slow-smoked over real charcoal to our rich, fall-off-the-bone oxtails. We believe food brings people together, and that's exactly what we do.
